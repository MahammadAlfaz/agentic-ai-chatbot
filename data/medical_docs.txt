Fever is a temporary increase in body temperature, often due to an infection.

Common symptoms of flu include fever, cough, sore throat, and fatigue.

Paracetamol is commonly used to reduce fever and relieve mild pain.

Ibuprofen is used for pain relief, inflammation, and fever reduction.

COVID-19 symptoms include fever, cough, and difficulty breathing.

Chest pain may indicate heart problems and requires immediate medical attention.
